1 About PubMan

PubMan supports scientists and institutes in the management and the digital curation of their publications. This solution addresses all disciplines and focuses on the target groups scientists, local librarians and local IT. Due to the heterogeneous policies, cultures and workflows of the Max Planck Institutes, customization and configuration options throughout the solution’s features are of major importance.

1.1 Basic Functionalities

For better overview, we divide the basic functionalities of the repository into three main areas of interaction with users and/or systems:

  • Submitting content
  • Managing content
  • (Re-)Using content

1.2 Basic concepts

For proper design of the solution and its features and alignment with conditions applied to other services, you find here an overview of our basic functional concepts.

  • Versioning
  • Control of Named Entities
  • Persistent Identification
  • Collections and Organizational Units
  • Metadata

2 Preface

2.1 Log in

Please enter your username and your password to make submissions to eSciDoc.

3 Search

Three different searches are provided.

  • Quick Search
  • Advanced Search
  • Organizations Search

3.1 Quick Search

Please enter one or more search terms and click on “ok” to perform a search.

The following boolean operators are supported:

  • AND
  • OR
  • NOT

If you want to search within the metadata and the full text file attached to a record, please click on the checkbox besides “Include Files”.

3.2 Advanced Search

You can either search via single search options (Any field, Persons, Organizations, etc.) or you can combine the search options with “And”, “Or” or “Not”.

3.3 Organizations Search

Here you can choose the Organization you are interested in and see the publications, that have been entered for that Organization.

4 Search results

If you clicked on “include full text files”, also records will be displayed where the search term was found within the attached full text. You can change the display of single items or of the whole list.

4.1 Export

You can either retrieve the selected items in a certain citation style (e.g. APA) or you can export the items to a certain database (e.g. EndNote). If you export the items to a layout format you additionally have the possibility to send that file via E-Mail.

4.1.1 Send Export via E-Mail

You can send Exports in one of the layout formats (PDF, RTF, ODT, HTML) per Mail. Therefor you have to specify the receiver of the E-Mail and the E-Mail address the receiver can reply to.

If you want to send the mail to more than one E-Mail address, please separate the addresses with a comma and a blank.

5 View item

5.1 Release History

Here you can see the different metadata versions of an item.

5.2 Item Revisions

Here you can find a list of the various revisions of an item.

5.3 View statistics of item

Here you can see how often the item was retrieved and how often the attached full text was downloaded.

Please select on collection to start your data entry.

6 Create / Edit Item

Please specify the document type (Genre) you want to describe before you start entering the publication data.

If you want to enter more than one Creator, please click on ’add’ to retrieve another submission block for entering information on a Creator.

Please give at least a document type, title and a creator. They are marked with a red star, because you can’t save or submit the item when you didn’t fill these fields.

7 Modify Item

Where as “pending” items can be edited by the owner until s/he is fine with the result, released items can only be changed by users, which have the privilege of a moderator. Moderators are responsible for the quality assurance of the item metadata and can therefore change released items, which is called “modify item”.

8 Validate Item

Please validate the item in order to check if it meets the requirements of the collection.

Please note, that you can’t submit an item, which doesn’t meet the requirements of the collection.

9 Save Item

If you go on the “save” button, your item is being put in the status “pending” and can only be seen by you. This might be useful if you want to complete your entry at a later time.

10 Save and Submit Item

If you go on “save and submit item”, your data will be validated. If your item is valid, you are guided to a mask, where you can state a comment to your submission.

10.1 Submission Comment

In the comment field you can give a comment on your submission, which the user, who is working on your item in the workflow, can read.

11 Accept Item

If you want to release the item, you have modified, please go on “accept”.

11.1 Acceptance Comment

In the comment field you can give a comment on accepting the item, which the user, who is working on the item in the further workflow, can read.

12 Delete Item

Please go on delete item, if you wish to delete the item. Only items in state “pending” can be deleted.

13 Create new Revision

Please state in the comment field the reason for the revision of the item.

14 Withdraw Item

Please state the reason for withdrawing the item.

15 Depositor Workspace

From this page you can start all actions, concerning the submission and management of your item(s) to eSciDoc. Furthermore you have the possibility to change the sorting of the items, that are displayed in your workspace and choose which records (item state) you want to have a look at.

15.1 Item State

Please open the pull-down list to view items from another item state.

15.2 Sort by

Please specify the sort option of your item list here by opening the pull-down list.

If you want to change the default sorting order (ascending, descending), please click into the radio button beside the desired sorting order.

15.3 New Submission

A submission mask will be opened when you click on this link.

15.4 Edit

Please select an item from your item list and click on “edit” to edit the item.

15.5 Submit

Please select one ore more items from your item list and click on “submit” to submit the item.

15.6 Delete

Please select one ore more items from your item list and click on “delete” to delete the item.
